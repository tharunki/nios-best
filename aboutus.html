What is NIOS?

The National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) is a government-recognized board that offers open and distance learning. It provides a flexible education system for students who cannot attend regular schools or want an alternative way to study.
NIOS, you can:
1.Choose your own subjects from a wide range of academic and vocational courses.
2.Study at your own pace without the pressure of fixed school hours.
3.Appear for exams when you feel ready, giving you more control over your learning.
4.Get certificates that are accepted by universities, colleges, and employers across India and abroad.
5.NIOS is perfect for students who want to continue their education but need flexibility due to work, family, or other reasons.
