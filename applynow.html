NIOS Registration Admission Open for Secondary(10th) and Senior Secondary(12th) Classes

Fill the registration form below to apply under NIOS board.

Note: Services Included: Registration Fees, Admission Fees, Examination Fees, Practical Fees, Assignment Fees, Book Fees and Support Fees.

Documents required for 10th Class Admission

  • 8th or 9th Class Pass school leaving certificate signed by the principal & Gazetted officer
  • Address Proof (Aadhar Card)/ Rent Agreement (if rented)
  • Caste certificate (if OBC, SC, ST category)
  • Disability Certificate (for handicapped)
  • Original failure/compartment mark sheet of class 10th (for TOC transfer of credit)
  • One latest coloured photograph (passport size)

Documents required for 12th Class Admission

  • Scan/Photocopy of 10th class passed mark sheet
  • Address Proof (Aadhar Card)/ Rent Agreement (if rented)
  • Caste certificate (if OBC, SC, ST category)
  • Disability Certificate (for handicapped)
  • Original failure/compartment mark sheet of class 12th (for TOC transfer of credit)
  • One latest coloured photograph (passport size)
